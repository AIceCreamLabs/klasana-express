PROJECT OVERVIEW

Background

Krabi's real estate market is experiencing rapid growth at 6.69% CAGR, yet no specialized digital platform exists for the sub-7M property segment. Current sellers rely on fragmented channels:

  • National portals (DDproperty, FazWaz) where Krabi is buried in massive databases
  • Facebook groups (unverified, scam-prone)
  • Local agents (high fees, limited reach)

Klasana Express solves this problem by creating Krabi's first dedicated platform for property sales, combining:

  • ✅ Free listings (removes seller friction)
  • ✅ Modern mobile-first UX (vs. dated national portals)
  • ✅ Krabi-only focus (deep vs. shallow)
  • ✅ Trust & verification (safe for buyers/sellers)
  • ✅ Multiple revenue streams (subscriptions, ads, transaction fees)
