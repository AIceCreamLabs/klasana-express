Market Gap

No specialized platform focuses solely on sub-7M Krabi properties. Existing national portals (DDproperty, DotProperty, FazWaz) treat Krabi as a minor segment. RE/MAX and local agents dominate through personal networks, not curated digital platforms.

Competitive Advantages

  • Hyper-local Krabi focus with sub-7M sweet spot (residential, villas, investment properties)
  • Free listings (solves barrier to entry vs. competitors)
  • Premium subscription for homepage visibility (recurring revenue without blocking listings)
  • Ad-driven revenue + transaction support fees (multiple revenue streams)
  • Brand exposure for Klasana (top-of-funnel marketing)
  • Mobile-first, modern UX optimized for sellers

3-Year Vision

Klasana Express becomes the default platform for buying and selling sub-7M properties in Krabi, capturing 30–40% of the secondary market within 18 months through trust, UX, and brand association.

Thailand Real Estate Market Context

Market Size & Growth

  • Thailand residential real estate market: USD 31.71 billion (2026), growing at 5.11% CAGR to 2031
  • Property classifieds market growing at 21.25% CAGR through 2032 (significantly faster than physical real estate)
  • Bangkok dominates 46.55% of market value; Phuket is growing at 6.69% CAGR (and Krabi tracks similarly)

Thailand-Specific Factors

  • Digitalization accelerating: 53.6 million Thais use internet (2021); projected 60 million by 2025
  • Smartphone penetration: 85%+ in urban areas, driving mobile-first design demand
  • Foreign buyer interest: Increasing, especially in Phuket, Krabi, Pattaya (10–15% of transactions)
  • Rental market growing: Rentals expanding at 6.41% CAGR; but Klasana Express focuses on sales only

Krabi-Specific Market Opportunity

Why Krabi?

  • Tourism-driven demand: Krabi is Thailand's second-largest tourist destination after Bangkok. Properties attract foreign investors for holiday rentals and long-term capital appreciation.
  • Price point: 1.5M–7M THB range captures mid-market local buyers, investors, and foreign expats (sweet spot for growth).
  • Development boom: Central Pattana's USD 125M Krabi complex (2025–2026), plus ongoing villa and condo launches.
  • No incumbent digital monopoly: Unlike Bangkok (heavy DDproperty/DotProperty penetration), Krabi lacks a dedicated platform.
  • Brand visibility: Klasana Express listing becomes Klasana's brand touchpoint with 3,000–10,000 monthly property hunters.

Market Size Estimation (Krabi)

  • Estimated 250–400 active listings across all platforms currently (fragmented)
  • Target: 500–800 listings within 6 months; 1,200–1,500 within 18 months
  • Average transaction value: 3.5M THB (mid-market, below 7M cap)
  • 20–30% of Krabi's annual secondary market capture = potential 2,000–4,000 annual listings at maturity

Technical Architecture & Stack

Recommended Stack

Given your profile as a full-stack developer with Firebase, Replit, and JavaScript/TypeScript expertise:

  • Frontend: Next.js (React framework) for SEO and performance
  • Backend: Firebase (Firestore for database, Storage for images, Authentication for users)
  • Hosting: Vercel (seamless Next.js deployment) or Firebase Hosting
  • Development: Start in Replit for rapid MVP prototyping, then migrate to production
  • Maps: Google Maps JavaScript API (you're familiar with this)
  • Payments: Stripe (international) + 2C2P or OmiseGo (Thai local)

This stack leverages your existing skills and allows 4-6 week MVP delivery.

Security & Trust Features
